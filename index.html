Help Center
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book a ticket on Narendra Travels? +
You can book online via our website or mobile app. Select your route, date, and preferred seat, then pay securely. You'll receive your e-ticket instantly via SMS and email.
Can I cancel or reschedule my ticket? +
Yes! If you cancel your ticket, you get a 70% refund to your original payment method. Alternatively, you can reschedule to any future date — your full ticket value transfers with ALL amenities (meals, entertainment, WiFi, seat preference) intact. No extra charges for rescheduling!
Is live tracking available for all buses? +
Yes! All our buses are equipped with GPS tracking. You can track your bus from our website or app using your booking ID. Alerts are sent automatically 30 and 15 minutes before arrival.
Are there ladies-only seats? +
Absolutely. We have designated ladies-only seats (marked in pink) on all our buses. Solo female travelers can book these for added safety and comfort. Our lady marshal service is also available on select routes.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and popular wallets. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.
What luggage is allowed on board? +
Each passenger is allowed one bag up to 15 kg in the luggage compartment and one small carry-on bag (up to 5 kg). Extra luggage can be arranged at a small charge by contacting us in advance.
